All dental check-ups are free of charge. If you require any further treatment, your dentist can advise you of the estimated cost and discuss treatment alternatives. Many people will automatically qualify for free NHS dental treatment on the basis of age, if you receive certain benefits, or if you are pregnant or a new mother. Our reception team will advise you of which circumstances entitle you to treatment free of charge. A list of the exemptions can be found here http://www.scotland.gov.uk/Publications/2011/03/30092604/46

Fees for NHS dental treatment are set by the Scottish Executive and as such are the same in every dental practice in Scotland. We ask that payment be made at each appointment for the work carried out on that day, and we accept cash, cheque or credit/debit card.
